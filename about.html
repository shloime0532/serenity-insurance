About Serenity Insurance

Your trusted partner for comprehensive insurance solutions

Who We Are

P&C Brokerage Group Inc, doing business as Serenity Insurance Brokerage, is a full-service insurance brokerage dedicated to helping businesses protect what matters most. We specialize in Property and Casualty insurance solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.

With years of experience in the insurance industry, our team brings deep expertise and a commitment to excellence in every interaction. We understand that navigating insurance can be complex, which is why we work closely with our clients to simplify the process and deliver coverage that truly fits their needs.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, reliable insurance solutions that give our clients peace of mind. We believe that every business deserves access to quality coverage and personalized service, regardless of size or industry.

Why Work With Us

Personalized Service

We take the time to understand your business, your risks, and your goals. Every client receives individualized attention and customized solutions.

Comprehensive Coverage

From general liability to specialized coverage, we offer a full range of Property & Casualty insurance products to protect every aspect of your business.

Industry Expertise

Our team has extensive experience working with businesses across multiple industries, from construction and real estate to retail and manufacturing.

Claims Support

When you need to file a claim, we're here to help. Our team provides guidance and advocacy throughout the entire claims process.

Responsive Communication

Questions don't wait for business hours. We pride ourselves on being accessible and responsive whenever you need us.

Carrier Relationships

We partner with multiple trusted insurance carriers, giving you access to competitive rates and flexible coverage options.
